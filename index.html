Unordered Smashketball Tier List

This is an unordered tier list for the game Super Smash Bros. Ultimate, in the gamemode "Smashketball."

Intro

Smash bros is a platform fighting game with a large character roster of 89 playable fighters. Smashketball is a fan-made game mode involving a custom stage, where the goal is to hit your opponent into the top corners of the stage, where they will go into a cannon, be shot downward, and die. The current meta-game for Smashketball are characters that can combo their opponent up and to the side, "up and over" combos, or characters that have strong attacks that send foes into the cannons.

Top tier

The best characters

  • Mii Brawler - Brawler has good "up and over" combos off of down throw into back aerial or forward aerial. Brawler can also consistently kill at 0 damage with down throw into Helicopter Kick.
  • Falco - Again, good "up and over" combos. Up throw and up tilt easily combo into F-air, N-air, and B-air.
  • Sora - Good combos of of down throw into F-air or N-air, which send at good diagonal angles.
  • Donkey Kong - Haha, funny Cargo throw go brrrrrrrrrrr. All of his Cargo throws send a really good angles.
  • Pikachu - Pikachu is already a fantastic character with an amazing combo game in normal play. They are definitely good in this mode.
  • Luigi - Same deal as Pikachu, except they have better combos, but aren't as good of a character.
  • Captain Falcon - Good combos, and has plenty of attacks to hit opponents into the cannons.
  • Ken/Ryu - They might be very different characters from each other, but they're about the same tier in Smashketball. They mostly have several easy to hit moves that send a very good angles, namely F-air.
  • Steve - "Up and over" combos. U-lilt into F-air or B-air are good go-to combos.
  • Joker - Mr. Top Tier himself. You shouldn't be surprised. Excellent combos, and is just a good character.
  • Meta Knight - Good "up and over" combos from U-air chains into neutral B, N-air, B-air, literally all of his moves.
  • Pyra/Mythra - Good "up and over" combos. For both characters
  • Palutena - Haha, funny N-air go brrrrrrrrrrr.
  • Zero Suit Samus - Up special hits at good angle; good combos.
  • Ike - Good combos. Landing n-air hits up, into F-air, B-air, or D-air which all hit to the side.
